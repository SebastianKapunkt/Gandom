Hi, we are the "Early Birds" and this site is the result of our group project for the course "JEE Web Technologies" in the summer semester of 2014 at Fachhochschule Brandenburg.

The idea was to make a site that collects all your video games and helps you to manage them.



But that's not all! Many gamers have huge video game collections. Not just 5 or 10 but hundreds. So what do you do with that many games? Right, you want to play them! But where do you start?

We can help you out here! Push the magic button and we select a random game from your collection for you.

Let's start playing and have fun!
