This site is a project from Early Birds. We are computer science students, and wrote in our course JEE-Webtechnologien this site.
The idea was to make a site that collect all your games to manage them.



But thats not all! A lot of people , not "normal" ones, have a lot a games. Not like 5 or 10, they have hundreds. So what you do with that bunch of games? Right you want to play them! But what game first? And this is the point we help you! We choose randomly a game that you can play!
